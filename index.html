Originating from Prince Edward Island, Mack's professional journey began in the maritime industry, progressing from laborious work on mussel and oyster farming vessels to self-directed pursuits. In his design philosophy, Mack seamlessly integrates simplicity with grandeur, employing a meticulous creative process comparable to crafting a refined masterpiece. Beyond graphic design, Mack demonstrates a versatile skill set, delving into realms such as comedy, music, and a deep appreciation for the wonders of nature, embodying an artist's mission to redefine the commonplace and elevate it to the extraordinary.
